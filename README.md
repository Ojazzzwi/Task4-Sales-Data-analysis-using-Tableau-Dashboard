# 📊 Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as **Task 4 – Dashboard Design** for the **Data Analyst Internship**.

The objective was to design an interactive business dashboard using **Tableau** that enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and regional trends through meaningful visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Design an interactive Tableau dashboard
- Visualize key business metrics
- Present actionable business insights
- Support data-driven decision making

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Tableau Public
- Kaggle Superstore Sales Dataset

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- 💰 Total Sales
- 💵 Total Profit
- 📦 Total Orders
- 📊 Profit Margin

### Visualizations
- 📈 Monthly Sales Trend
- 🌍 Profit by Region
- 🥧 Sales by Category
- 📊 Sales by Segment
- 🏆 Top 10 Products

### Interactive Features
- Region Filter
- Category Filter
- Segment Filter
- Dynamic Dashboard Interactions
---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Total Sales exceeded **$2.29 Million**.
- Total Profit reached approximately **$286K**.
- Profit Margin was approximately **12.5%**.
- The **Consumer** segment generated the highest sales.
- The **West** region contributed the highest profit.
- Monthly sales displayed an overall upward trend from 2014 to 2017.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing investment for high-performing products.
- Develop strategies to improve sales in lower-performing regions.
- Optimize inventory planning using monthly sales trends.
- Continue investing in the Consumer segment while exploring growth opportunities in Corporate and Home Office segments.
